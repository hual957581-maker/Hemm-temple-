## 🎨 Design Elements

- **Color Scheme**: Saffron (#FF9933), Red (#CC0000), Gold (#FFD700), White (#FFFFFF)
- **Typography**: Sacred/devotional fonts for seva names, clear fonts for amounts and descriptions
- **Icons**: 💰 Money/Coin, 🙏 Namaste, 🍽️ Food Plate, 📿 Prayer Beads, 🕯️ Diya
- **Interactive Features**: Amount selection buttons, payment method toggles, expandable seva details
- **Visual Enhancements**: Progress bars for donation goals, impact statistics, security badges
