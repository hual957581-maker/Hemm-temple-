## 🎨 Design Elements

- **Color Scheme**: Saffron (#FF9933), Red (#CC0000), Gold (#FFD700), White (#FFFFFF)
- **Typography**: Clear, readable fonts for chat and information
- **Icons**: 📺 TV/Monitor, 🔴 Live Indicator, 💬 Chat Bubble, 🔔 Bell, ⏰ Clock
- **Interactive Features**: Real-time chat, viewer count, stream controls, reminder settings
- **Visual Enhancements**: Live status indicators, thumbnail previews, responsive video player
