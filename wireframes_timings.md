## 🎨 Design Elements

- **Color Scheme**: Saffron (#FF9933), Red (#CC0000), Gold (#FFD700), White (#FFFFFF)
- **Typography**: Clear, readable fonts with traditional Devanagari elements
- **Icons**: 🔔 Bells, 🕯️ Diyas, 🕐 Clock, ⏰ Alarm, 📅 Calendar
- **Interactive Features**: Live clock, reminder toggles, expandable cards, video player
- **Visual Enhancements**: Progress bars for time until next aarti, color-coded timing cards
