## 🎨 Design Elements

- **Color Scheme**: Saffron (#FF9933), Red (#CC0000), Gold (#FFD700), White (#FFFFFF)
- **Typography**: Devanagari script for headings, serif fonts for historical content
- **Icons**: 🏛️ Temple, 🕉️ OM, 🙏 Namaste, 📿 Prayer Beads, 🕯️ Diya
- **Visual Elements**: Timeline graphics, quote cards, image galleries with lightbox
- **Interactive Features**: Expandable sections, image zoom, audio recitation (optional)
