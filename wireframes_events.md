## 🎨 Design Elements

- **Color Scheme**: Saffron (#FF9933), Red (#CC0000), Gold (#FFD700), White (#FFFFFF)
- **Typography**: Festive fonts for festival names, clear readable text for details
- **Icons**: 🪔 Diya Lamp, 🌸 Lotus, 🙏 Namaste, 📅 Calendar, 🔔 Bell
- **Interactive Features**: Clickable calendar dates, expandable event details, image galleries
- **Visual Enhancements**: Color-coded festival categories, countdown timers, festive imagery
