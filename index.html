About Shri Khedapati Sarkar Mandir

🏛️ History & Heritage

श्री खेड़ापति सरकार मंदिर is a sacred temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman, located in the heart of Madhya Pradesh. This divine abode has been serving devotees for generations, spreading the message of devotion, strength, and faith.

🕉️ Deity Information

Shri Hanuman Ji (Khedapati Sarkar)
The presiding deity of our temple, Lord Hanuman, is revered as the symbol of strength, courage, and unwavering devotion. Known as Khedapati Sarkar, He blesses devotees with protection and spiritual growth.

"हनुमान जी की कृपा से सभी विघ्न दूर हो जाते हैं।"
"By Lord Hanuman's grace, all obstacles are removed."
