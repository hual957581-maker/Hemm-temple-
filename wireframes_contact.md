## 🎨 Design Elements

- **Color Scheme**: Saffron (#FF9933), Red (#CC0000), Gold (#FFD700), White (#FFFFFF)
- **Typography**: Clear, professional fonts for contact information and forms
- **Icons**: 📞 Phone, 📧 Email, 🗺️ Map, 💬 Chat, 📍 Location, 🕐 Clock
- **Interactive Features**: Interactive map, WhatsApp chat widget, contact form with validation
- **Visual Enhancements**: Contact cards, staff profiles, FAQ accordion, location highlights
